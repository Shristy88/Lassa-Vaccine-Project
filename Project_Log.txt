------------------------------------------------------------

Date: 2026-07-05

Phase 5: Immunological Profiling
------------------------------------------------------------

Date: 2026-07-05
Tool: IEDB Analysis Resource
Method: Comparative binding affinity analysis across common human HLA alleles; focused on "promiscuous" binding potential.

Status: Complete.

----------------------------------------------------------
Phase 6: Structural Validation
----------------------------------------------------------

Date: 2026-07-05
Tool: RCSB PDB (Mol* Viewer)
Method: In-silico 3D epitope mapping onto the Lassa virus glycoprotein (PDB ID: 5VK2) to verify solvent-surface accessibility.
Result: Confirmed that selected epitopes are structurally stable and exposed on the protein surface, suitable for immune recognition.
Status: Complete

Tool: MS Excel
